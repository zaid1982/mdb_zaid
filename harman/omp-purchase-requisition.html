Purchase Requisition

Objective

This policy provides guidelines for the purchase requisition of company motor vehicle, building, fixed asset, office supplies, marketing and promotional materials to ensure that all purchases for the company is appropriate and value for money.

Scope and Responsibilities

These policy and procedure apply to all employee, Office Management, Finance Dept and HODs.

Procedures (for all purchases except for training project purchases)
  1. The requester to get the quotation approved as per the delegation of authority and pass the approved quotation to the Office Management Department for issuance of Purchase Requisition.
  2. The Office Management will keep a copy of approved quotation for referral for future purchase.
  3. The Purchase Requisition attach with the approved quotation and invoice submit to Finance Department for approval of payment.
  4. If approval done on the quotation/Invoices, no signature required on the Purchase Requisition.
  5. All Purchase requisition must be prepared within 24 hours upon receiving approved quotation/invoice.
