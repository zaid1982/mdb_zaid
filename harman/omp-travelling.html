Travelling

Objective

This policy sets out the Company’s policy and procedures regarding the Company’s policy on official Duty and the reimbursement of expenses incurred by employees during the performance of their duties as employees.

This policy is intended to facilitate prompt reimbursement of reasonable and appropriate business expenditure incurred directly by an employee of the Company and that reimbursement will be made directly to the personal bank account.

Scope and Responsibilities

It is the employee’s responsibility to ensure that travel and expenditure is both reasonable and necessary for the conduct of the Company’s business and complies with the provisions of this policy. Employees should familiarise themselves with this policy prior to incurring expenditure.

Policies
  1. MODE OF TRAVEL

    Employee who are required to travel for official duty can choose the following mode of transport:

    • By Air
    • Own Vehicle
    • Public Transport
    1. Travel by Air
      1. All travel arrangement by air shall be made by the Company and shall be by Economy class.
      2. All air travel booking shall be authorized and verified by the HOD.
      3. All air travel shall be covered by travel insurance.
      4. The Company will pay for the check-in baggage up to 20 kilos if necessary.
      5. All air travel shall be covered by travel insurance.
      6. For domestic flight, in-flight food & beverage is not provided.
      7. For overseas flight, in-flight food & beverage is provided as follows:
        • Limited to RM 20.00 per trip for traveling from 2 hours to 4 hours
        • Limited to RM 30.00 per trip for traveling from 4 hours and above
      8. Pre-book seating is under personal expenses unless with reasonable justification.
      9. In order to optimize cost savings, employees should make travel arrangements at least 7 days in advance before the travelling date for domestic travel and 14 days in advance for international travel.
    2. Travel by own vehicle (motorcar / motorcycle)
      1. If two persons or more required to travel for the same destination, sharing car is compulsory. Only one person is eligible to claim the mileage expenses.
      2. Mileage claim reimbursement as per Company mileage rate.
      3. Parking & Toll will be reimbursed as per receipt.
      4. Receipt/s of expenses to be attached with the Mileage Claim Form.
      5. Motorcycle is not allowed for outstation traveling.
    3. Travel by public transport
      1. All travel by public transport shall be reimburse at cost.
    4. Travel from Home to Airport
      1. Public transport may be used for round trip transportation from employee’s home to the airport and return if the cost of airport parking plus mileage rate equals a greater amount then the round trip of taxi or public service.
  2. VISAS AND PASSPORT

    Employees traveling internationally should check that their passport with at least six (6) months validity and a 4 blank visa pages. Employees may never travel on a tourist visa when traveling on business but rather obtain a business visa (or equivalent) as necessary prior to the scheduled departure date.

  3. ACCOMMODATION
    1. Employee who are required to overnight for official duty shall be eligible for hotel accommodation.
    2. For domestic official duty, the maximum hotel rate of RM200.00 per day
    3. The Company only responsible to pay for hotel room only
    4. Other charges will be under personal account
Procedures
It is the employee’s responsibility to ensure that travel and expenditure is both reasonable and necessary.
  1. OUTSTATION/OVERSEAS TRAVEL
    1. Employees must complete and submit the Traveling Request Form (Appendix 1) with approval of HOD to Office Management for air ticket and accommodation booking and another requirement together with the approved project costing sheet.
    2. Office Manager to book air ticket and hotel accommodation according to the Travelling Requisition Form by using online booking through company’s credit card.
    3. Once the payment is made, Office Manager to print the invoice for air tickets and invoice for room accommodation to issue Purchase Requisition.
    4. Purchase requisition submitted to Finance Department for processing and payment.
  2. CLAIMS
    1. Where claims for reimbursement shall be done in the “Expenses Claim Form”.
    2. All Claim must be approved by the Head of Division.
    3. To submit your claim to Administrator two working days before deadline submission to Finance by 20th of the month This is to give time to Administrator for checking, verification and approval by Division Head.
    4. To claim all expenses incurred on the same month. This is to make sure all expenses accurately costed in on the same month and to close job files timely.
    5. Late submission of claim will result the claim to be paid on the next following month.
    6. Accumulation of claim will only be allowed for extension of one month otherwise claim will be forfeited.
    7. All receipts must be attached with claim form. Claim without receipt will be rejected.
