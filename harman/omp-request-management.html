Request of Management

Objective

When a position has become vacant due to a departmental needs and / or an incumbent’s retirement, resignation, change in assignment, transfer, promotion, demotions, dismissal, the Head of Division will initiate the recruitment process to fill the position vacancy. The purpose of this procedure is to ensure compliance with the Company policy and consistency and timeliness in filling position vacancies.

Scope and Responsibilities

This policy covers all the vacant across the functions, levels & hierarchy. Heads of Division (HOD) to collaborate with Office Manager (OM) and Head of People (HOP) to initiate the hiring process at any point of time.

Policy
  • The Company is committed to support of this objective the importance of employing the most suitable applicant for all vacant positions
  • The Company will ensure it has the best opportunity to attract the best available staff by broadly advertising (internally and externally as deemed appropriate) all vacancy.
  • The Company will take all reasonable steps to ensure that applicants may be safely entrusted with the duties of their position
  • The Company will internally advertise all vacant positions to current staff to encourage career advancement and increase participation
  • All requirement and selection procedures and decision will reflect the Company’s commitment to providing equal opportunity by assessing all potential candidates according to their skills, knowledge, qualification and capabilities. No regards will be given to factors such as age, gender, marital status, race, religion, physical impairment or political opinions.
Procedures
  • HOD to prepare the Job Description for the vacant position
  • HOD to fill-in the Manpower Requisition Form (MRF)
  • HOD to get the MRF & Job Description to be approved by the CEO
  • The approved MRF and Job Description submit to OM
  • OM will collaborate with HOP for sourcing of candidates
Requirement Quality

In today’s knowledge driven business scenario, People are perceived as the most valuable assets of an organization and the optimum utilization of the skill, knowledge, attitude, they possess, are directly instrumental to the growth of any organization.

Therefore, while recruiting a candidate for any role, position, level, function, it should always be ensured that there is no compromise in the quality of people, we hire.

Besides checking the presence of role-specific key competencies & the behavioral attributes required to perform a job, few basic eligibility criteria should be considered, even before a candidate is called for the Initial rounds of Interviews.

Requirement Sources


1. Internal Recruitment

As a conscious focus of the organization to nurture high potential talents by providing them suitable career growth opportunities within the organization, efforts would always be made to fill in specific vacancies from its existing human resource pool.

The entire process would be done through Internal Job Posting (IJP) and communication including the job profile, candidate profile, eligibility (who can apply), application deadline etc. would be made available by HOP.

Employees possessing necessary skills, knowledge, experience matching with those required for the job may apply through the appropriate communication channels as prescribed in the IJP.

2. Other Recruitment

To ensure a steady in-flow of quality candidates for all the existing vacant positions, with an aim to select the best within a stringent recruitment deadline, OM & HOP would always focus to develop a robust database of CVs searched from the following sources:

2.1 Hiring Recruitment Agency

Hiring recruitment agency is another source of recruitments to be done within a small span of time, it is necessary to identify and engage recruitment agency to source quality candidates for middle and senior level positions.

Process guidelines (To engage a new Hiring Recruitment Agency):

  • OM & HOP would identify the recruitment agency in all the regions, based on their current clients’ distribution, database size, past performance records & industry feedback.
  • OM & HOP will negotiate the Terms & Conditions with all the identified consultants and will get a one-time approval from the CEO before rolling out the formal agreement with them.

Quality Expectations from the Hiring recruitment agencies:

  • Minimum Turn-around time (TAT): Once a requirement is placed, the recruitment should forward at least 6 CVs within the next 5 working days.
  • Strong Conversion Rate (6:4:2): Out of the 6 CVs forwarded by a consultant, 4 have to be short-listed (after initial screening by OM and Head of People) and at least 2 of them have to be selected.

An Annual Evaluation of the services provided by the existing consultants across the country would be done to create a list of preferred recruitment agencies, who would be treated as priority CV sources for critical positions in future.
