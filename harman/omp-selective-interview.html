Selective & Interview

Objective

The Company is committed to employing, in its best judgment, the best qualified candidates for approved company positions while engaging in recruitment and selection practices that are in compliance with all applicable employment laws. It is the policy of the Company to provide equal employment opportunity for employment to all applicants and employees.

Scope and Responsibilities

Office Manager & Head of People and Head of Divisions are responsible for the overall management of the selection process.

Policy
  • Interview Session must be conduct by HOD with the present of Head of People or Office Manager
  • The final interview with key position such as Sales, Consultant and Finance must be attended by CEO and HOD
Procedures
  • Upon receiving resumes from the recruitment sources, if the resume received by Office Manager, the resume to be forwarded to HOP for consolidation and Head of People submit the resumes to HOD to identify the most appropriate candidates for interviewing.
  • HOD to inform HOP on the selected candidates for interview session.
  • The Head of People will conduct telephone pre-screens of identified candidates and arrange for interview session via phone or email. Interview must be conducted within 5 working days upon selection from HOD.
  • Once the interview date and time is set, Head of People to inform HOD the interview session and send the soft copy of the following documents to the candidates:
    • Job Application Form
    • Intensive Business English test
  • Head of People will advise the candidates to complete the above forms and to bring along during the interview together with other related documents such as qualification certificates.
  • On the interview date, the Interviewer/s to collect the Job Application Form and Business English test from the candidate.
  • After the interview session, the interviewer/s to fill in their assessments of the candidate/s on the last page of the Job Application Form.
  • If the candidate is unsuccessful, the Application Form and other related documents to hand over to OM for issuance of letter of rejection within 7 working days after the interview.
  • If the candidate is chosen for 2nd interview with CEO, Head of People will conduct reference and background check of the candidate before the interview. HOP to inform HOD the result of the reference and background check. The final interview with CEO must be arrange within 5 working days after the 1st interview session.
