Attendance Management

Objective

This policy aims to encourage a positive approach to attendance with particular emphasis on support, and free communication between employees and their managers. It also aims to ensure that all employees are treated in a fair and consistent manner whilst enabling the Company to continue to run effectively.

Scope and Responsibilities

This Attendance Policy and Procedure applies to all employees. An employee becomes aware of their inability to attend work for any reason, they should notify their manager or office manager as soon as possible but no later than one hour after their normal start time,

Procedures
  • All employee must be registered with Scan Face Attendance system and Swingvy Leave Management system when they start work with the company.
  • All employee must clock in when reporting to work and clock out after finish work at the scan face attendance system.
  • All employee must apply their leave through Swingvy Leave System.
  • Every morning office management to update staff attendance record file by checking the scan face clock in report and swingvy leave management report.
  • Every 21st of the month, office management to submit the summary attendance record to Finance Dept. for salary calculation.
  • Every month, office management to submit summary of employee attendance to the respective HOD.
