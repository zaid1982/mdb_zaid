On Boarding of New Employee

Objective

To ensure new employees are assisted in adjusting to the Company’s work requirement and to learn more about the Company.

Scope and Responsibilities

Head of People, Office Manager and the HOD Division are responsible to make sure the on boarding program will make the new employee become familiar with their new surrounding and her roles and responsibility.

Policy
  • All new employee must undergo the on-boarding program and to completed within 3 – 6 months during the probationary period.
  • HOD must conduct at least 2 times performance review with new employee during their probationary.
Procedures
  1. Before the employee’s starting date
    1. The Office Manager to prepare the following:
      • Offer Letter
      • Non-Disclosure Agreement
      • Non-Compete Agreement (applicable for position of Sales, CTC and Trainers)
      • New Employee Check list Form
      • New Employee Registration Form
      • EPF Registration Form for 1st time working employee
      • Acknowledgement of Company Property Form
      • Employee official contact and office extension list
      • To create Email
      • Work Station
      • Stationeries
      • Laptop/desktop/handphone
      • Business Card (if applicable)
      • Office Access Card
    2. The Head of People will prepare the onboarding file that consist:
      • Orientation program
      • Orientation material and presentation
    3. The Head of Department will prepare:
      • Job Description
      • Job Training Schedule
      • Performance Planning Key Result Area (As per appendix)
  2. On Commencement Date
    1. The Head of People will make a welcome announcement by email or company WhatsApp.
    2. The new employee should submit and signed the following documents on the date of joined to OM:
      • Signed and accepted copy of the offer letter
      • A copy of IC
      • New Employee Registration Form
      • Acknowledgement Form of Company properties
      • Non-Disclosure Agreement
      • Non-Compete Agreement (applicable to Sales, CTC and Trainers)
    3. Office Manager to create new employee ID and record in the “Employee list file” and create ID for face scan system and leave system.
    4. Office Manager to scan the New Employee Registration Form to Finance Dept. and cc to Associate Director of CAFOM.
    5. Office Manager to open employee personal file and file all the related employment documents in the employee personal file.
    6. OM make sure the New Employee Hire Check list is completed and sign by the HOD. Once completed file in the Employee personal file.
    7. All personal documents are Private & Confidential, only authorized personal is allowed to access the file.
    8. The Personal file cabinet must be lock at all time.
