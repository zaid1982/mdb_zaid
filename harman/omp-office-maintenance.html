Office Maintenance

Objective

The objective is to outlines the policy and procedures for requesting maintenance, service, or repair for the Company office equipment.

Scope and Responsibilities

Office Manager is responsible to ensure the policy is follow through according to guidelines. This procedure cover the maintenance and repair of air conditioning, moveable office equipment and other building maintenance.

Procedures
  1. Repair of computer equipment
    • Staff to inform Office Management on the faulty computer.
    • If under warranty, Office Management will coordinate the repair with the vendor.
    • If the computer is not covered by a warranty, Office will coordinate with the Company outsourcing IT to find out what is the course of faulty and may elect to have the repairs performed by and outside vendor or manufacturer.
    • Office Management to request quotation for the repairs and to be approved by Head of CAFOM.
    • Office management to prepare Purchase Requisition and duly approved for payment.
    • If the cost of repair is too costly, the outsourcing IT to recommend the next course of action.
  2. Repair of moveable office equipment
    • If under warranty, Office Management to coordinate with the vendor for repair.
    • If the equipment is not covered by a warranty, office management will coordinate the repairs performed by and outside vendor or the manufacturer.
    • To request quotation for the repair and approved by Head Of CAFOM.
    • To prepare Purchase Requisition and duly approved and submit to Finance for payment.
    • If the cost is too costly or the equipment is unrepairable, Office Manager to recommend the next course of action.
  3. Repair and service maintenance of Air-Con, Pest Control, office Hygiene
    • To sign a service maintenance agreement with selected vendor and to be approved by CEO.
    • The agreement to be sign by the Head of CAFOM.
    • A copy of signed agreement to be submitted to Finance Dept.
    • When the services are done, Office Management will submit the invoice together with the service note to Finance Dept for payment.
  4. Repair and service maintenance of office building
    • All repair and maintenance related to electrical required an immediate action.
    • Office Management will call the Company outsourcing electrical vendor for repair.
    • When the repairs are done, office management will submit the invoice (approved by Head of CAFOM) together with the service note to Finance Dept for payment.
