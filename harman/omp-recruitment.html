Recruitment

Objective

The aim of the policy is to outline the relevant steps in the recruitment process and to assign individual responsibilities for each of these steps.

Scope and Responsibilities

Head of People, Office Manager and HODs to ensure that all aspects of recruitment are carried out in a proper and efficient.

Policy
  • Offer letter must be duly signed by CEO
Procedures
  1. Once the candidate is selected, HOD and CEO to approve the salary, position and other conditions in the last page of the Application Form and hand over to the HOP.
  2. HOP to extends the offer to the candidate via standard email that indicate the position, salary and other terms and conditions with cc loop the OM.
  3. Once the candidates agree to the proposals & intimate their acceptance, HOP will forward the Approved Application Form to OM to prepare the Offer Letter within 3 days after the acceptance of offer.
  4. Offer letter check list:
    Office Manager should make sure that all the below-listed documents are received & checked thoroughly before issuing the formal offer letters –
    • Approved Manpower Requisition form (MRF)
    • Job Application Form with the CEO Approval
    • Resume (hardcopy) and other related documents of the candidate
  5. OM to send via email the duly signed offer letter to candidates to acknowledge receipt on the acceptance.
Recruitment of Expatriate

Recruitment of expatriate can only be done with the approval from Immigration Dept.

Recruitment Cycle Time

To bring in more dynamism and effectiveness in the recruitment process, Office Manager and Head Of people would follow a deadline of 30 days (from the day it had received the approved Manpower Requisition) to hire a new employee.
