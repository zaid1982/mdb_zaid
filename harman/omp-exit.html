Exit (Voluntary Resignation)

Objective

The aim of the policy is to outline the relevant steps in the voluntary resignation process and to assign individual responsibilities for each of these steps.

Scope and Responsibilities

All Employees, Office Manager, Head of Division and Head of People.

Procedures
  1. Once employee decided to resign from their employment with the Company, they must submit in writing resignation letter to the Head of Department and CC to the Office Manager.
  2. Upon receiving the letter of resignation, the HOD to acknowledge receipt by signing on the letter with date of received.
  3. HOD to meet the employee within 3 working days upon receiving the resign letter and discuss with the employee.
  4. If the employee decided to continue the employment, OM will print the meeting recap confirmation and file together with the resignation letter.
  5. If employee decided to resign, OM to print the re-cap meeting and check last working day, balance of leave entitlement and other information that required for issuance of acceptance of resignation letter.
  6. The acceptance letter attached together confirmation meeting recap and the resignation letter to be verify by the HOD and duly signed by Associate Director of CAFOM.
  7. The duly signed acceptance letter to be surrender to the employee latest by 7 working days after confirmation of meeting recap.
  8. OM to inform Finance Dept on the employee resignation by indication the following information:
    • Name of Employee
    • Last Working day
    • Balance leave entitlement
    • Any other deduction
  9. After issuance of acceptance letter, if employee decide to continue the employment, he/she to write in officially to retract the resignation to the HOD and cc loop to HOP & OM.
  10. Upon receiving the retract letter, HOD to discuss employee with the present of HOP and send out the meeting recap to the employee and cc loop the HOP & OM. Employee to acknowledge the meeting recap.
  11. If the Company agree to accept the retract letter, OM to issue cancellation of acceptance of resignation letter. The letter to be verify by HOD and duly signed by Associate of CAFOM.
  12. Office Manager will inform via email to Finance Department on the retraction letter.
  13. Head of people arrange and meet the employee for exit Interview and submit the Exit interview report to the CEO and Office Manager.
  14. Head of People to do post-mortem if any grievances were made during the exit interview and submit the report to the CEO.
  15. The Head of Division to make arrangement on the job transfer and hand over to the successor. The job transfer and hand over must consist of the following:
    • List of Job responsibilities/Task
    • Completed/On-going/Pending Job status
    • Client base information
    • Files/proposal (soft & hard copy & password)
    • Drawers & Cabinet keys
    • Introduction with clients
    • Any other information related to the job responsibilities
    • Laptop password
    • Clients names cards
  16. All job transfer and hand over must be signed by the employee and HOD.
  17. On the last day of work, the employee to hand over all company properties to Office Manager.
